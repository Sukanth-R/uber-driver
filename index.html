Driver Guidelines

Drive
Safely

Always follow traffic rules, drive responsibly, and ensure the safety of passengers during every ride.

Be On
Time

Reach the pickup location quickly and avoid unnecessary delays to provide a better experience for passengers.

Verify Passenger

Confirm the passenger's name and destination before starting the ride to avoid confusion.

Professional Behavior

Maintain polite communication with passengers and provide a friendly travel experience.

Maintain Vehicle

Keep your vehicle clean and well maintained to ensure comfort and safety for passengers.

Avoid Ride Cancellation

Do not cancel rides unnecessarily after accepting them unless there is an emergency.
